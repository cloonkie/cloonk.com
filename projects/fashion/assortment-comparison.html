Assortment Comparison — Appendix

What this is

Assortment Comparison is a browser-based overlap tool for retailer assortments. Upload a workbook, choose the retailers to compare, switch the product key, and inspect which styles are shared or unique. The spreadsheet stays local to your browser.

Quick start

1. Click Template for a starter workbook.
2. Load a sheet with product and retailer/account columns.
3. Select up to five retailers, choose one or more brands, and pick whether comparison metrics use Unit Sold, Unit OH, or Sold + OH.
4. Click any Venn count or segment row to drill into that overlap, then Export View.

Autosave

The latest uploaded comparison, selected retailers, selected brands, metric mode, filters, and active segment are saved in this browser with IndexedDB and restored when the page opens again.

Product key modes

Style is broadest. Style + Color, Style + Color + Grid, and Brand + Style + Color + Grid progressively treat variants as more distinct.

Glossary

Hover any term for a plain-language explanation.

TermWhat it means
RetailerThe account, customer, store group, or banner carrying the product. This is equivalent to KAC in the Power BI/R example. The account whose assortment is being compared.
StyleThe product identifier counted as a style in the overlap. Depending on the selected key, a style can include color, grid, and brand. The product-level count used in each overlap.
Unit SoldUnits sold for that retailer/product row. Select Unit Sold as the metric when the overlap should be weighted by sales movement. Sales quantity summed into the comparison.
Unit OHCurrent on-hand inventory for that retailer/product row. Select Unit OH when the overlap should be weighted by remaining inventory. On-hand quantity summed into the comparison.
Sold + OHA combined metric that adds Unit Sold and Unit OH for each row before aggregating the overlap. Useful for total footprint comparisons. Combined sales and on-hand footprint.
OverlapA product belongs to an overlap when the same product key appears under multiple selected retailers. Products carried by more than one selected retailer.
UniqueA product that appears under exactly one selected retailer for the current key and filters. Products carried by only one selected retailer.
