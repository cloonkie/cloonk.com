    Selling Analysis — Appendix

    What this is

    A browser-based diagnostic for the YTD Sell-Out workbook. It cleans the raw dump, flags integrity issues, then surfaces liquidation risk, velocity, momentum, and promo behaviour over a filterable canonical record set.

    Story-first

    The page opens to a table of reads. Picking one shapes the rest: only that story's parameters are foregrounded, the file dropzone asks for the workbook in that story's voice, and the hero metric promoted in the vitals strip is the one that matters most for that read.

    Privacy

    All parsing happens locally in your browser. The workbook is never uploaded to a server.

    01Cover

    A table of every read the tool knows about. The question under each title is what that read answers — pick the one that matches yours.

    02Story prelude

    Once a story is chosen, the prelude shows the dropzone and the parameters that affect this read (promo threshold, aged-before). It also lists the metrics the read wants — useful before you upload.

    03File landed

    After the workbook is parsed, the prelude collapses. The vitals strip appears and the hero KPI for this read is promoted full-bleed. The story body fills in.

    04Move between reads

    The footer of every story leads to the next or previous read; the meta strip leads back to the cover. Filters live in a drawer so they never push the article out of focus.

    Required workbook shape

    The tool scans the top of the workbook for the best header row, including clustered cadence rows above metric headers. Each story runs only when its required fields are present.

    Integrity flags

    Rows with one of the flags below are excluded from the metrics they would distort, but stay in the integrity counts.

    • MSRP missing / zero — excluded from AUR, promo, and markdown depth.
    • Negative units / retail / on-hand — excluded from sales totals, AUR, and inventory health.
    • ST% out of range — excluded from the velocity matrix.
    • AUR above MSRP — excluded from promo penetration so above-list rows do not pull the share down.

    Velocity matrix

    Rows are classified by a median split on ST% and TY units. Star = high ST × high volume; Sleeper = high ST × low volume; Slow-bleeder = low ST × high volume; Dog = low ST × low volume.

    Momentum decomposition

    YoY unit variance per brand is split into New (TY only), Dropped (LY only), and Continuing (both years). Continuing carries the delta; New is additive and Dropped is subtractive.
